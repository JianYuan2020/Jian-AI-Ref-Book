Discussion on Getting More Data¶

  1. Make sure you have a low bias classifier (so more data will help) before expending the effort. (Plot learning curves). E.g. keep increasing the number of features/number of hidden units in neural network until you have a low bias classifier.

  2. “How much work would it be to get 10x as much data as we currently have?”

    • Artificial data synthesis

    • Collect/label it yourself

    • “Crowd source” (E.g. Amazon Mechanical Turk)
